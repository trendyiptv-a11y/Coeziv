Formula BSS
ΔV-score = 100 − (ΔV/120mV)×100 (tăiat la 0..100).
ΔT-score = 100 − (ΔT/10°C)×100 (0..100).
I-score = 100 − (|Ipeak|/50A)×100 (0..100).
BSS = 0.50×ΔV-score + 0.30×ΔT-score + 0.20×I-score.
